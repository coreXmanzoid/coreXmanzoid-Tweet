Privacy Settings

Control how your profile appears to others.

*Your account settings give you full control over your privacy and visibility. You can decide what others can see about you, including your personal details and activity. Adjust these settings anytime to match your comfort level and how you want to interact with others on the platform.

*When this option is enabled, your account becomes private and only approved followers will be able to see your posts, stories, and profile details. New users will need to send you a follow request, and you can choose whether to accept or ignore it. This helps you maintain better control over your audience.

*This setting allows you to control whether your birthdate is visible on your profile. Keeping it visible can help friends remember your special day, while hiding it adds an extra layer of privacy and protects your personal information from being publicly accessible.

*Your bio is a short description that tells others about you. Enabling this option will display your bio on your profile, helping people understand your interests or personality. If you prefer to keep things minimal or private, you can choose to hide it at any time.

*Allow others to see when you are currently online or when you were last active. This can make conversations feel more real-time and engaging. However, if you disable this feature, your activity status will remain hidden, and you will also not be able to see the online status of other users.

Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to manage your account with confidence, from profile updates to privacy controls.

How can I reset or change my password?+
Open the Change Password section, enter your current password, then create and confirm a new one. For the best protection, choose a password that is unique to this account and includes a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
How do I make my account more private?+
Visit the Privacy Settings area and turn on options like Private Account, hide your birthdate, or limit profile details you do not want shown publicly. These controls let you decide exactly what other people can see.
What is Manzoid-AI and how does it work? +
Manzoid-AI is our built-in intelligent assistant designed to help you generate content, get quick answers, and enhance your overall experience on the platform. Simply open the AI bar, type your request, and Manzoid-AI will respond instantly with helpful suggestions and insights.
What is the easiest way to update my profile picture?+
In the Profile section, choose Upload New, select an image, and use the built-in crop tool before saving. A clear square image usually gives the best result across the app.
What are the benefits of upgrading to Pro? +
With a Pro subscription, you get access to advanced features including premium content creation tools, the most powerful version of Manzoid-AI, and priority performance. Pro users also receive a verified blue tick, monetization opportunities, and many more exclusive features designed to enhance your experience.
