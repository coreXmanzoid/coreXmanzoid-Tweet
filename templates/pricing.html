Pricing & Plans FAQ

Everything you need to know about Free, Pro, and Enterprise plans—features, limits, and upgrades.

What is included in the Free plan? +
The Free plan gives you access to core features like limited monthly flicks, basic profile customization, and access to Manzoid-AI with restrictions. It’s perfect for exploring the platform before upgrading.
What extra benefits do I get with the Pro plan? +
The Pro plan unlocks higher limits, advanced content tools, full access to Manzoid-AI, and a verified badge. You’ll also enjoy an ad-free experience and better overall performance.
Who should choose the Enterprise plan? +
The Enterprise plan is designed for power users, teams, and businesses. It offers maximum limits, priority support, advanced analytics, multi-device access, and exclusive premium features.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime? +
Yes, you can switch between Free, Pro, and Enterprise plans at any time. Changes take effect immediately or at the next billing cycle, depending on your subscription.
Will I lose my data if I change my plan? +
No, your data is always safe. However, some features may become limited if you downgrade to a lower plan.
Is there a free trial for Pro or Enterprise? +
Depending on current offers, we may provide free trials for Pro or Enterprise plans. Check the pricing page for the latest availability.
