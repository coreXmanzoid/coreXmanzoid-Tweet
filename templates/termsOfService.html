ChatFlick is a text-based microblogging platform. Posts are limited to 180 characters on the frontend. Comments are limited to 130 characters. You may include hashtags (prefixed with #) and mention other users (prefixed with @).

✓ Permitted

  • Original opinions, commentary, and personal updates
  • Hashtags, @mentions, and links to external content
  • Reposting and liking content from other users
  • Editing or deleting your own posts within 3 minutes of creation

✗ Prohibited

  • Hate speech, harassment, or targeted abuse
  • Spam, unsolicited advertising, or repetitive content
  • Impersonating other users, public figures, or coreXmanzoid

Hashtags are extracted from post content and stored separately for discovery purposes. Editing a post does not re-parse hashtags or mentions — the original extracted values are retained.

Engagement Features

ChatFlick provides likes, reposts, shares, and comment likes to enable social interaction. By using these features you agree to the following:

  • Post likes and reposts are tracked per user — you can only like or repost a given post once. Counters are floored at zero.
  • Share counts are incremented per action and are not uniquely tracked per user — the same user can increment the share counter multiple times.
  • Comment likes are not uniquely enforced per user at the backend. You agree not to exploit this to artificially inflate comment counts.
  • Engagement actions on your own content (self-like, self-repost, self-share) are technically possible but do not affect milestone notifications sent to yourself.

Milestone notifications (at 100, 1,000, and 10,000 likes, reposts, and comments) are triggered automatically by the platform. You cannot opt out of milestone notifications for your own content.
