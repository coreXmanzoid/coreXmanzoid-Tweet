### **UI/UX Tips**

* **Organize by tabs or sections** – avoids overwhelming the user.
* **Use toggles for simple on/off settings** (privacy, notifications).
* **Provide confirmations** for sensitive actions (password change, account deletion).
* **Show “last updated” timestamps** for changes.
* Keep **mobile-friendly design** in mind with collapsible sections.
* Include **save/cancel buttons** at the bottom or inline.
